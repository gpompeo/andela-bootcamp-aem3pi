# Commercial Agent Documentation

## FAQ — Subscription plans

We offer three subscription tiers: Basic, Professional, and Enterprise. Each plan includes different feature sets and support levels.

## Knowledge Base — Upgrade process

To upgrade your plan, log into the customer portal, navigate to “Manage Subscription,” and select the desired tier. Upgrades take effect immediately.

## SOP — Downgrade request

Customers wishing to downgrade must submit a request via the portal. The system escalates the case to human support for confirmation and retention efforts.

## Policy — Cancellation terms

Cancellations require 30 days’ notice. Customers remain responsible for charges incurred during the notice period. Data export is available before termination.

## FAQ — Free trial

New customers can access a 14-day free trial of the Professional plan. No credit card is required. At the end of the trial, users can upgrade or cancel.

## Knowledge Base — Pricing calculator

The portal includes a pricing calculator to estimate monthly costs based on user count and add-ons. Discounts apply for annual billing.

## SOP — Escalation to support

If a customer indicates intent to cancel, the agent must escalate to human support. Escalation ensures retention strategies are applied.

## Policy — Discounts

Volume discounts apply for contracts with more than 100 users. Non-profit organizations may qualify for special pricing. Discounts must be approved by Sales.

## FAQ — Payment methods for subscription signup

We accept credit cards, PayPal, and wire transfers for subscription signup. Invoices are generated automatically for Enterprise customers during initial signup and renewal.

## Knowledge Base — Add-ons

Available add-ons include advanced analytics, premium support, and API access. Add-ons can be purchased individually and billed monthly.

## SOP — Renewal process

Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled. Customers receive a reminder email 15 days before renewal. Renewal invoices are generated on the renewal date.

## Policy — Refunds

Refunds are only issued for billing errors. Service dissatisfaction does not qualify for refunds. Exceptions require VP approval.

## FAQ — Contract length

Contracts are available monthly or annually. Annual contracts include a 10% discount. Customers can switch contract length at renewal.

## Knowledge Base — Customer portal

The customer portal allows users to manage subscriptions, billing, and support tickets. Access requires account credentials.

## SOP — Handling cancellations

When a customer cancels, confirm data export options, disable auto-renewal, and escalate to human support. Document the reason for cancellation.

## Policy — SLA

Enterprise customers receive a 99.9% uptime SLA. SLA credits apply if downtime exceeds thresholds. SLA terms are outlined in contracts.

## FAQ — Upgrade timing

Upgrades take effect immediately. Customers are billed pro-rata for the remainder of the billing cycle.

## Knowledge Base — Promotions

Promotions are announced quarterly. Customers can apply promo codes during checkout. Promo codes cannot be combined with other discounts.

## SOP — Customer onboarding

After purchase, customers receive a welcome email with setup instructions. Agents ensure onboarding materials are delivered within 24 hours.

## Policy — Reseller agreements

Resellers must comply with company pricing and branding guidelines. Violations may result in termination of reseller status.

## FAQ — Multi-currency pricing

Pricing is available in USD, EUR, and BRL. Exchange rates are updated monthly. Customers can select currency during checkout.

## Knowledge Base — Customer support tiers

Basic: email support only. Professional: email + chat. Enterprise: email, chat, and phone support. Support tiers align with subscription plans.

## SOP — Handling upgrade requests

Verify customer eligibility, confirm billing details, and process the upgrade in the portal. Notify the customer of changes.

## Policy — Data retention after cancellation

Customer data is retained for 90 days after cancellation. After that, data is permanently deleted. Customers can request early deletion.

## FAQ — Annual billing

Annual billing offers a 10% discount compared to monthly billing. Customers can switch to annual billing at renewal.

## Knowledge Base — Customer success program

Enterprise customers are assigned a Customer Success Manager. The manager provides quarterly reviews and strategic guidance.

## SOP — Processing refunds

If a billing error occurs, submit a refund request to Finance. Refunds are processed within 10 business days.

## Policy — Trial limitations

Trials include full access to features but limited to 10 users. Trials cannot be extended beyond 14 days.

## FAQ — API access

API access is included in Enterprise plans and available as an add-on for Professional plans. Documentation is provided in the developer portal.

## Knowledge Base — Feature comparison

The portal includes a comparison chart of features across Basic, Professional, and Enterprise plans. Customers can use this to evaluate options.

## SOP — Customer feedback

Collect feedback during cancellations or downgrades. Submit feedback to the Product team via the feedback portal.

## Policy — Early termination

Early termination of annual contracts incurs a 25% fee on remaining contract value. Exceptions require executive approval.

## FAQ — Invoicing

Invoices are generated automatically for Enterprise customers. Professional and Basic customers can download invoices from the portal.

## Knowledge Base — Customer lifecycle

The customer lifecycle includes onboarding, adoption, expansion, and renewal. Agents are trained to support each stage.

## SOP — Escalating billing issues

If a customer disputes a charge, escalate to Finance. Document the issue and provide supporting evidence.

## Policy — Customer data usage

Customer data is used only for service delivery and support. Data is not shared with third parties without consent.

## FAQ — Downgrade timing

Downgrades take effect at the end of the billing cycle. Customers retain access to higher-tier features until then.

## Knowledge Base — Customer retention strategies

Retention strategies include discounts, feature demos, and success manager engagement. Agents should apply strategies before cancellation.

## SOP — Processing add-on requests

Verify customer eligibility, confirm billing, and activate the add-on in the portal. Notify the customer of activation.

## Policy — Customer communication

All customer communication must be professional and documented. Agents must use approved templates for emails.

## FAQ — Support availability

Support is available 24/7 for Enterprise customers. Professional customers receive support during business hours. Basic customers receive email support only.

## Knowledge Base — Customer escalation paths

Escalations follow a defined path: Agent → Manager → Customer Success → Executive. Escalation ensures timely resolution.

## SOP — Handling promotions

Verify promo code validity, apply discount, and confirm billing. Document the promotion in the customer record.

## Policy — Customer satisfaction

Customer satisfaction is measured via quarterly surveys. Agents are evaluated based on satisfaction scores.

## FAQ — Feature requests

Customers can submit feature requests via the portal. Requests are reviewed by Product quarterly. Agents should encourage submissions.

## Knowledge Base — Customer advocacy

Advocacy programs include case studies and testimonials. Customers can volunteer to participate. Advocacy strengthens brand reputation.

## SOP — Customer renewals

Confirm renewal terms, verify billing, and process renewal in the portal. Notify the customer of renewal confirmation.

## Policy — Customer complaints

Complaints must be documented and escalated to management. Agents must respond within 24 hours. Complaints are tracked for resolution.

## FAQ — Billing cycles

Billing cycles are monthly or annual. Customers can view billing dates in the portal. Changes require manager approval.

## Knowledge Base — Customer journey mapping

Journey maps outline customer interactions across touchpoints. Agents use maps to identify pain points and opportunities.

## SOP — Customer upgrades

Verify eligibility, confirm billing, and process upgrade. Notify customer of changes. Document upgrade in CRM.

## Policy — Customer loyalty

Loyalty programs reward long-term customers with discounts and perks. Eligibility is based on tenure and contract value.
