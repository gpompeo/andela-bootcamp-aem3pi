# Finance Agent Documentation

## FAQ — Payment processing and terms

We process payments via credit cards, PayPal, and wire transfers. Enterprise customers may request invoicing with net-30 payment terms. For questions about available payment methods during subscription signup, contact Commercial.

## Knowledge Base — Billing cycles

Billing occurs monthly on the 1st or annually on the contract start date. Customers can view billing dates in the portal.

## SOP — Processing refunds

Submit refund requests via Finance > Refunds. Include invoice number and reason. Refunds are processed within 10 business days.

## Policy — Late payments

Payments overdue by more than 15 days incur a 2% late fee. Accounts may be suspended after 30 days.

## FAQ — Invoice retrieval

Invoices are available in the customer portal under Billing > Invoices. Enterprise customers receive invoices via email.

## Knowledge Base — Expense categories

Expenses are categorized as Travel, Meals, Equipment, or Training. Categories ensure compliance with accounting standards.

## SOP — Submitting expenses

Employees submit expenses via Finance > Expenses. Upload receipts and select categories. Manager approval is required.

## Policy — Travel reimbursement limits

Meals are reimbursed up to BRL 150 per day. Travel expenses must comply with company travel policy. Exceptions require Finance approval.

## FAQ — Currency support

We support USD, EUR, and BRL. Exchange rates are updated monthly. Customers can select currency during checkout.

## Knowledge Base — Payment status

Payment statuses include Pending, Paid, and Overdue. Customers can track status in the portal. Finance updates statuses daily.

## SOP — Approving budgets

Managers submit budget requests via Finance > Budgets. Finance reviews within 5 business days. Approved budgets are tracked quarterly.

## Policy — Audit compliance

All financial records are subject to annual audits. Employees must cooperate with audit requests. Non-compliance may result in disciplinary action.

## FAQ — Tax receipts

Tax receipts are available for all payments. Customers can download receipts from the portal. Enterprise customers receive receipts via email.

## Knowledge Base — Financial reporting

Monthly financial reports are generated automatically. Reports include revenue, expenses, and profit. Managers can access reports in the portal.

## SOP — Processing payroll

Payroll is processed on the 25th of each month. Finance verifies hours and deductions. Employees receive payslips via the HR portal.

## Policy — Travel advances

Travel advances are available for trips over BRL 5,000. Advances must be requested 15 days before travel. Unused funds must be returned.

## FAQ — Refund eligibility

Refunds are issued only for billing errors. Service dissatisfaction does not qualify. Exceptions require VP approval.

## Knowledge Base — Budget tracking

Budgets are tracked quarterly. Managers can view budget utilization in the Finance portal. Overages require Finance approval.

## SOP — Vendor payments

Submit vendor invoices via Finance > Vendors. Finance processes payments within 15 business days. Vendors receive remittance advice via email.

## Policy — Corporate card usage

Corporate cards must be used for business expenses only. Personal use is prohibited. Violations may result in card revocation.

## FAQ — Payment reminders

Customers receive payment reminders 7 days before due date. Overdue reminders are sent daily until payment is received.

## Knowledge Base — Expense approval workflow

Expenses require employee submission, manager approval, and Finance verification. Workflow ensures compliance and accuracy.

## SOP — Processing reimbursements

Employees submit reimbursement requests with receipts. Finance verifies and processes within 10 business days. Payments are made via payroll.

## Policy — Financial confidentiality

Financial data is confidential. Employees must not share data outside the company. Violations may result in disciplinary action.

## FAQ — Wire transfer details

Wire transfers require IBAN, SWIFT code, and beneficiary details. Customers can find instructions in the portal.

## Knowledge Base — Payment gateways

We use Stripe and PayPal for online payments. Gateways ensure secure transactions. Customers can choose preferred gateway.

## SOP — Approving invoices

Managers review invoices in the portal. Approved invoices are processed by Finance. Payments are made within 15 business days.

## Policy — Expense audits

Finance audits expenses quarterly. Employees must provide receipts and documentation. Non-compliance may result in reimbursement denial.

## FAQ — Payment disputes

Customers can dispute payments via Finance > Disputes. Finance reviews disputes within 10 business days.

## Knowledge Base — Subscription billing

Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled. Customers can view billing details in the portal. Finance manages renewals.

## SOP — Processing cancellations

When a customer cancels, Finance verifies outstanding payments. Refunds are processed if applicable. Accounts are closed after settlement.

## Policy — Vendor contracts
