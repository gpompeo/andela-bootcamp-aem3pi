# HR Agent Documentation

## FAQ — Paid time off (PTO) accrual

Employees accrue PTO monthly based on tenure:

- 1–2 years: 1.5 days
- 3–5 years: 2 days
- 6+ years: 2.5 days  
  Unused PTO rolls over up to 10 days per calendar year.

## Knowledge Base — Requesting vacation via portal

Use the HR portal: My Time > Request PTO. Select dates, choose “Vacation,” add an optional note, and submit. Your manager gets an approval task; you’ll receive an email confirmation once approved.

## Policy — Sick leave eligibility

Full-time employees receive 10 sick days per year; part-time employees receive prorated amounts. Sick leave can be used for personal illness or medical appointments. A doctor’s note is required after the third consecutive day.

## FAQ — Maternity leave

Maternity leave is 16 weeks: 10 paid, 6 unpaid but job-protected. Paid portion uses the maternity leave plan and does not reduce PTO. Notify HR at least 12 weeks before expected due date.

## SOP — Submitting a sick note

Scan or photograph the doctor’s note clearly. Upload to HR portal: My Documents > Medical > Add. Tag the related absence dates; HR reviews within 2 business days.

## FAQ — Paternity leave

Eligible employees receive 4 weeks paid paternity leave within six months of the child’s birth or adoption. Leave can be taken consecutively or split into two blocks. Submit the request at least 4 weeks in advance.

## Knowledge Base — Payroll calendar

Payroll runs on the 25th of each month; cut-off for changes is the 15th. If the 25th is a holiday/weekend, payroll is processed the prior business day. Calendars are posted in the HR portal under Resources.

## Policy — Overtime compensation

Non-exempt employees are paid 1.5× for hours over 40 in a week. Overtime must be pre-approved by managers. Exempt employees do not receive overtime pay but may take compensatory time when approved.

## FAQ — Changing tax withholdings

Update your tax forms in the HR portal: Payroll > Tax Forms. Changes take effect next payroll cycle. Consult a tax advisor for personal guidance; HR provides general support only.

## SOP — Address change

Navigate to Profile > Personal Information. Update address and upload proof of residence (utility bill). HR validates within 3 business days; payroll updates occur automatically.

## Knowledge Base — Health insurance enrollment

Open enrollment is in November for coverage starting January 1. New hires have 30 days from start date to enroll. Compare plans in the Benefits hub; use the coverage estimator to model typical costs.

## Policy — Remote work eligibility

Employees can work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval. International remote work requires HR/legal review. Equipment stipends are provided annually per the IT policy.

## FAQ — Holidays

We observe 12 company holidays, including New Year’s Day, Carnival Monday/Tuesday, Good Friday, Labor Day, Independence Day, Proclamation of the Republic, Christmas, and two floating holidays. Floating days must be used within the same calendar year.

## SOP — Reporting workplace injury

Seek medical attention immediately. Inform your manager and file an incident report in the HR portal within 24 hours. HR will coordinate with Safety and Legal for next steps.

## Knowledge Base — Probation period

Standard probation is 90 days for all new hires. Performance check-ins occur at 30, 60, and 90 days. Successful completion triggers eligibility for full benefits and bonus programs.

## Policy — Performance review cycle

Annual reviews run in June with mid-year check-ins in December. Ratings use a 5-point scale with defined competencies. Calibration sessions ensure fairness across teams.

## FAQ — Bonus eligibility

Bonuses are tied to company performance and individual ratings. Employees rated 3+ are eligible; 4–5 may receive multipliers. Contractors are not eligible unless specified in contract.

## SOP — Internal job application

Apply via Careers > Internal Roles. Notify your current manager after submitting. HR ensures process confidentiality; interviews occur during business hours with minimal disruption.

## Knowledge Base — Learning stipend

Employees have an annual BRL 3,000 learning budget. Reimbursable items include courses, certifications, and relevant books. Submit receipts within 30 days of purchase.

## Policy — Code of conduct

Treat colleagues and customers with respect. Harassment, discrimination, and retaliation are prohibited. Violations may result in disciplinary action up to termination.

## FAQ — Employee referral bonus

Referrals hired into full-time roles yield a BRL 5,000 bonus after 90 days. Submit referrals through the HR portal with the candidate’s resume. Bonus is taxable.

## SOP — Expense reimbursement

Use Finance > Expenses in the portal. Upload receipts, select categories, and submit within 30 days. Manager approval is required; Finance pays out within 10 business days.

## Knowledge Base — Attendance tracking

Clock in/out via the HR app or web portal. Missed punches must be corrected the same day. Persistent issues may trigger a performance discussion.

## Policy — Flexible hours

Core hours are 10:00–16:00 BRT. Flex within 07:00–19:00 BRT with manager approval. Ensure cross-timezone coverage for customer-facing teams.

## FAQ — Bereavement leave

Employees receive up to 5 paid days for immediate family, 2 days for extended family. Additional days may be approved case-by-case. Coordinate with HR for documentation.

## SOP — Travel approval

Submit proposed travel in the portal: Travel > Request. Include itinerary and estimated costs. Approval requires manager and Finance sign-off for budgets over BRL 10,000.

## Knowledge Base — Diversity and inclusion

We run quarterly D&I workshops and maintain employee resource groups. Inclusion metrics are reviewed annually. Report concerns anonymously via the Ethics line.

## Policy — Social media

Do not disclose confidential information. Personal accounts must state views are personal when referencing the company. Marketing handles official company channels.

## FAQ — Work equipment

Standard kit includes laptop, headset, and external monitor. Replacement due to wear-and-tear is covered; loss or negligence may incur cost-sharing. Request via IT ticketing.

## SOP — Return from leave

Notify HR at least two weeks before returning. Confirm role, schedule, and any accommodations. Update benefits if needed based on life event changes.

## Knowledge Base — Grievance process

File a grievance in the HR portal; choose anonymous or named. HR acknowledges within 2 business days and investigates within 10. Outcomes are documented and shared appropriately.

## Policy — Drug and alcohol

Prohibited on company premises and during work hours. Company-sponsored events may serve alcohol with limits; misconduct is not tolerated. Seek help via EAP.

## FAQ — Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

EAP offers confidential counseling and support services. Employees and immediate family can access 24/7. Contact details are in the HR portal under Wellbeing.

## SOP — Parental status update

Add dependent details under Benefits > Dependents. Upload birth certificate or adoption documents. Update within 30 days to avoid coverage gaps.

## Knowledge Base — Travel policy highlights

Economy class for flights under 6 hours; premium economy allowed over 6 hours with approval. Hotel caps vary by city; use preferred vendors. Per diem rates are in the Finance section.

## Policy — Anti-bribery and corruption

Gifts over BRL 300 must be declared. Facilitation payments are prohibited. Training is mandatory annually for commercial roles.

## FAQ — Salary review timing

Salaries are reviewed annually in June alongside performance reviews. Market adjustments may occur off-cycle for critical roles. HR communicates changes by July payroll.

## SOP — Remote work setup reimbursement

Submit receipts for ergonomic chairs, desks, and peripherals. Max BRL 2,000 per year. IT must approve compatibility for devices.

## Knowledge Base — Commuter benefits

On-site employees can claim public transit subsidies. Submit monthly passes or receipts. Benefits are taxable per local regulations.

## Policy — Whistleblower protection

Employees reporting misconduct in good faith are protected from retaliation. Reports can be anonymous. Investigations follow standardized protocols.

## FAQ — Visa sponsorship

We sponsor specific roles based on critical skills and budget availability. Eligibility considers tenure and performance. Contact HR for timelines and documentation.

## SOP — Performance improvement plan

Managers initiate PIPs for employees with performance concerns. Plans last 60–90 days with weekly check-ins. HR monitors progress and documents outcomes.

## Knowledge Base — Wellness programs

We offer gym subsidies, mindfulness apps, and quarterly wellness challenges. Participation is voluntary but encouraged. Rewards include gift cards and recognition.

## Policy — Data privacy

Employee data is stored securely and used only for HR purposes. Access is restricted to authorized personnel. Breaches are reported immediately to Legal and IT.

## FAQ — Relocation assistance

Eligible employees receive moving stipends and temporary housing support. Packages vary by role and destination. HR provides relocation vendor contacts.

## SOP — Exit interview

HR schedules exit interviews for departing employees. Topics include feedback, reasons for leaving, and benefits continuation. Notes are anonymized for trend analysis.

## Knowledge Base — Career development

Employees can access mentorship programs and career coaching. Internal mobility is encouraged. Resources are available in the Learning hub.

# Knowledge Base — Remote work setup

To request remote work, submit a “Remote Work” request in the HR portal under My Work > Requests. Include preferred days, justification, and confirm you have a stable internet connection. Your manager reviews and approves; HR records the arrangement in your profile.

## Policy — Parental leave eligibility

Parental leave is available to full-time employees with at least 12 months of continuous service. Eligible employees (including adoptive parents) may take up to 16 weeks: 10 paid and 6 unpaid but job-protected. Notify HR at least 12 weeks before the expected start date.

## SOP — Onboarding checklist

HR schedules orientation during the first week; IT provides hardware and accounts before day one. Managers share a role-specific training plan by day three. Employees must complete compliance training within 30 days. HR conducts a check-in at 30, 60, and 90 days.

## Policy — Overtime compensation

Non-exempt employees receive 1.5x their hourly rate for approved hours over 40 in a workweek. Overtime must be pre-approved by a manager. Exempt employees are not eligible for overtime pay but may receive compensatory time at the manager’s discretion.

## FAQ — Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

The EAP offers confidential counseling, financial guidance, and legal consultation. Employees and immediate family can access up to six sessions per year at no cost. Contact the EAP via the HR portal or the dedicated hotline listed in My Benefits.
